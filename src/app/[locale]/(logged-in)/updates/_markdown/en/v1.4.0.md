## Update v1.4.0 - English language support

Hello, fellow international buddies!!
The whole website should be completely translated to english for you guys.
If that's not the case - you now can change the default language on **[Settings > Language](/en/settings/language)**.

When MPost was made, i was thinking of my IRL friend group and people i knew stricly IRL -
but a lot of time has passed, and i made a bunch of online friends from around the world!
And recently when i put MPost up and running, it made me realize how it needed that english
language support. People would use browser auto-translate features which would result in
inconsistent translations.

So after a while, here it is!! I'm sorry it took a bit of time, but locked in REAL good for this.
